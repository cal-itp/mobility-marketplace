Established in 2021, California Mobility Marketplace makes it easy to browse and purchase statewide pre-approved contracts for new transit technologies to better serve riders.

Mobility Marketplace is a direct response to the challenges small agencies face in navigating the procurement process. The site breaks down all the necessary steps to help your agency succeed—from real-time data to establishing a contactless fare system. You can also get in touch with our Customer Success team, real people, who will support you every step of the way.

Mobility Marketplace is owned and operated by Cal-ITP through support from the California State Transportation Agency↗ and the California Department of Transportation↗. For more information about Cal-ITP, visit calitp.org↗.
